Faith at Work — Being a Light in the Marketplace

Colossians 3:23

Devotion:

The workplace is more than just where we earn a paycheck, it’s a powerful place of purpose. Whether in an office, classroom, hospital, shop, or at home, God calls us to bring His presence into every part of our work life.

Living out our faith at work doesn’t require a title or a platform. It shows up in the small things: how we speak to others, how we handle pressure, how we carry ourselves when no one is watching. Every email, meeting, task, or conversation can become an opportunity to reflect Jesus.

Being a disciple of Christ in the workplace means choosing excellence, walking in integrity, and treating every person with kindness and respect. It’s about letting God’s Spirit guide us in decisions, attitudes, and responses; especially when it’s difficult or inconvenient.

Prayer and purpose go hand in hand. Start your day by inviting God into your schedule, your relationships, and your responsibilities. Let His peace shape how you lead, follow, serve, and speak. You may be the only glimpse of Christ someone sees today. Your work can be worship. Your attitude can be ministry. Your job can become a mission.

Prayer:

Lord, thank You for the work You’ve placed in my hands. Help me to honor You through every task and interaction. Let my life reflect Your light and love in the way I serve others. Make my workplace a space of peace, purpose, and integrity. Use me to be an example of Your goodness, wherever I go. In Jesus’ name, amen.
