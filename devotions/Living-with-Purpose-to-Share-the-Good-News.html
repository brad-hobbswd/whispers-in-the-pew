Living with Purpose to Share the Good News

Matthew 28:19–20

Devotion:

All who follow Christ are called to share Him. Evangelism is not only for pastors or missionaries. Jesus clearly commanded His followers to take the Good News to others. What God has done in us is meant to reach beyond us.

Often, sharing our faith does not look like standing on a platform or giving a sermon. Instead, it shows up in everyday life through kindness, patience, listening, and honest conversation. A life lived with love and integrity speaks loudly and opens hearts to Christ.

Every day, God places people in our path. At home, at work, in our neighborhoods, and even among strangers, opportunities appear. Our responsibility is not to have perfect words or deep explanations. Our responsibility is simply to be willing and attentive.

Only God changes hearts. Our role is to share, to care, and to plant seeds. As we live and speak with faith, God does the rest.

You carry the light of Christ. Allow it to be seen in your words, your actions, and your presence. Your testimony matters, and someone is waiting to hear it.

Prayer:

Lord, thank You for the gift of salvation and the hope I have in You. Give me boldness to share that hope with others. Open my eyes to the opportunities around me. Let my life reflect Your love and let my words point to Your truth. Use me to make disciples, not in my strength, but by the power of Your Spirit. In Jesus’ name, amen.
