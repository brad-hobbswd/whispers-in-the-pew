Courage & Calling

Stepping forward when faith leads

God doesn’t choose the most qualified; He equips the willing. Courage isn’t the absence of fear; it’s the decision to move forward in faith when fear whispers “turn back.” These devotions speak to the heart of your calling: to stand firm in truth, to love boldly, and to serve with compassion even when it costs something.

You’ll meet moments of testing, confrontation, and surrender; and in each one, discover that courage is not something you create; it’s something God forms within you. Step into these stories ready to be reminded that your calling has purpose, and your obedience carries eternal weight.
